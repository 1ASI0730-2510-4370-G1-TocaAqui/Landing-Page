About Us

TocaAquí is a platform that connects emerging musicians with bars, clubs and event promoters. We facilitate direct booking, venue management and promotion of musical events in one place.

Our platform allows venues to publish available dates and receive band applications according to style, location and affinity. Musicians can schedule performances, sign digital contracts and receive secure payments.

We automate key processes such as technical validation, shared scheduling, digital ticket generation and post-event evaluation, creating a seamless and secure experience for artists and organizers.
